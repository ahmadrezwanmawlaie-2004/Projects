Diamond

Diamonds are one of the most precious gemstones, symbolizing luxury, strength, and timeless beauty. Formed under intense pressure, they are renowned for their brilliance and durability. Used in jewelry and industry, diamonds are valued based on cut, clarity, color, and carat weight, making them highly sought after.

Gold

Gold is a precious metal valued for its rarity, beauty, and versatility. It is widely used in jewelry, currency, and technology due to its resistance to tarnish and corrosion. Measured in karats, with 24K being the purest, gold symbolizes wealth and prestige, making it one of the most sought-after metals worldwide.

Silver

Silver is a precious metal known for its brilliance, conductivity, and versatility. Used in jewelry, currency, and industry, it is valued for its affordability and durability. Though prone to tarnish, it remains a symbol of elegance. Measured in purity, sterling silver (92.5%) is the most common, making it a timeless and sought-after metal.
